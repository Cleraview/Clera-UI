## **Components: flexible building blocks**

Our components are **primitives designed for reuse and flexibility**. They adapt to different contexts while maintaining consistency across applications. Explore how core elements like **buttons, cards, and forms** enable teams to design and develop faster without sacrificing usability or accessibility.

Consistency is achieved through **design tokens** such as colors, typography, and spacing. These tokens ensure that when you use a button or a card, they not only look consistent across pages but also respond predictably to changes in themes or brand identity updates.
Flexibility comes from the ability to **customize and extend components** without breaking their foundation. Whether you need a primary button, a ghost button, or a link-styled button, you can use the same base component and adjust it through variants and props, ensuring scalability for growing product needs.

Our components are **tested and documented** to reduce guesswork during implementation. Storybook examples, usage guidelines, and best practices are provided to help designers and developers collaborate more effectively and speed up decision-making during the design and development process.
