    ## **Purpose and Context**

    The `BrandLogo` component is used to consistently display the brand's logo throughout the application. It helps reinforce brand recognition and provides a visual anchor for users.

    In {config.title}, the `BrandLogo` component can be customized through its props:

    -   **Size**: Adjust the logo size to fit different contexts (e.g., header, footer, sidebar).
    -   **Variant**: Choose a visual style (e.g., color, monochrome) to match the background and overall design.
