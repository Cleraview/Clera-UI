    ## **Purpose and Context**

    Drawers are used to present supplementary information or actions without navigating away from the current page. They are commonly used for:

    -   Navigation menus
    -   Settings panels
    -   Filter options
    -   Detailed views of items

    In {config.title}, drawers are customizable through their props:

    -   **Open**: Controls whether the drawer is visible or hidden.
    -   **Position**: Determines the side of the screen from which the drawer appears (left, right, top, bottom).
    -   **FullScreen**: Makes the drawer occupy the entire screen.
    -   **Duration**: Sets the animation speed for the drawer's appearance and disappearance.
    -   **Title**: Provides a heading for the drawer's content.

    ## **FAQ**

    #### **How do I control whether the drawer is open or closed?**

    Use the `open` prop to specify whether the drawer is visible or hidden.

    <Canvas.Source>
      <Source className="my-0!" dark code={`
        <Drawer open={true}>Drawer Content</Drawer>
      `} />
    </Canvas.Source>

    <br />

    #### **How do I change the position of the drawer?**

    Use the `position` prop to specify the side of the screen from which the drawer appears. Options are `left`, `right`, `top`, and `bottom`.

    <Canvas.Source>
      <Source className="my-0!" dark code={`
        <Drawer position="left">Drawer Content</Drawer>
      `} />
    </Canvas.Source>

    <br />

    #### **How do I make the drawer full screen?**

    Use the `fullScreen` prop to make the drawer occupy the entire screen.

    <Canvas.Source>
      <Source className="my-0!" dark code={`
        <Drawer fullScreen>Drawer Content</Drawer>
      `} />
